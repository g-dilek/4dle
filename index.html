Welcome to 4dle!

by Grace

How to play:

  • Guess the 4-letter word - you have infinite tries.

  • Each guess gives feedback for each letter:
    • letter → Correct letter in the correct spot
    • * → Correct letter, wrong spot
    • _ → Incorrect letter

  • Try to solve it in as few guesses as possible!
  • Press Enter or click Guess to submit
